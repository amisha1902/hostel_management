Our Rooms

Indrayani rooms offer a diverse range of features and amenities to cater to the varying needs and preferences of our guests. From different room types accommodating solo travelers to families, our rooms are equipped with various bed configurations, private bathrooms, and additional comforts such as air conditioning and heating. Guests can enjoy Wi-Fi connectivity, entertainment options, and the convenience of minibars or kitchenettes in select rooms.
